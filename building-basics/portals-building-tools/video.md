# Video

### Overview

The Video tool allows you to display and play MP4 video files within your space. Useful for presentations, trailers, or environmental storytelling.

### Inventory Settings

#### Video Upload Interface

* Add URL Here – Paste a direct video URL (MP4 format).
* Drag & Drop – Upload a local MP4 file (max size: 100MB).
* Save – Confirms your selection and loads the video into your inventory.

### Placement Settings

#### Video Settings Panel

* Frame (Toggle) – Enable or disable a border around the video screen.
* Emission (Slider) – Adjusts the brightness/glow of the video display.
* Sound Falloff Start – Distance (in meters) where audio begins to reduce.
* Sound Falloff End – Distance where audio becomes completely silent.
* URL (Read-only) – Displays the current video URL in use.

### Common Use Cases

* Show trailers, cutscenes, or announcements
* Host virtual watch parties
* Add ambient motion or themed media to your space
