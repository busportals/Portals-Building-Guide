# Image

### **Overview**

The Image tool lets you display a static image or animated GIF as a framed wall-mounted art piece in your space. Perfect for personalization, branding, or decoration.

### Inventory Settings

Wall Painting Upload Interface

* Add URL Here: Paste a direct image or GIF URL to use an online asset.
* Drag & Drop: Upload an image or GIF directly from your device
* Save: Confirms the image selection and places it in your inventory.

### **Placement Settings**

Image Settings Panel

* Frame (Toggle): Turns the frame border around the image on or off.
