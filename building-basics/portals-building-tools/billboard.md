# Billboard

### Overview

The Billboard tool displays an image or GIF on a large sign structure. Choose from multiple sign shapes to fit your design needs. This tool is ideal for ads, announcements, memes, or decorative signage.

### Inventory Settings

#### Billboard Upload Interface

* Paste Image URL – Load an image or GIF from a direct link
* Drag & Drop – Upload a local image or GIF (max size: 1.5MB)
* Browse Files – Select a file from your device
* Choose Billboard Shape – Scroll and select from a variety of preset billboard models
* Spawn Sign – Places the selected billboard in your space

### Placement Settings

#### Image Settings Panel

* Emission – Controls how brightly the image glows
* Shadow On – Toggles whether the sign casts a shadow
* Size – Scales the overall size of the billboard
* Height – Adjusts the vertical height of the billboard structure
* Choose Custom – Opens the color picker for picking the sign border color

### Common Use Cases

* Signage for events, shops, or custom locations
* Display memes, art, or branding
* Directional or thematic environmental signage
