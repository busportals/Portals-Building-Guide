# Adding an Admin to your Space

### How to add another admin to your space:

1. Go to your Spaces Dashboard
2. Click on the space's settings icon
3. Click on the settings tab
4. Enter a wallet or email address
5. Enter an admin nickname
6. **IMPORTANT: Click Add Wallet+**
7. **IMPORTANT: Click Save**
