# December 2025 - Christmas Building Competition

## Overview

Build a Christmas-themed game in Portals and earn an exclusive, limited-edition Christmas wearable.

***

### Timeline

Build window: December 2, 2025 – December 23, 2025

***

### Participation & Rewards

* Who can enter: Anyone!
* What you build: A Christmas-themed game of your own design.
* Reward: Every participant who makes an honest effort (see below) and submits a qualifying game will receive an exclusive Christmas wearable (details to be revealed).

***

### Game Requirements

To qualify for the wearable, your submission must:

1. Be Christmas-themed
   * The core idea, visuals, or story should clearly relate to Christmas (e.g. snow, gifts, Santa, lights, winter vibes, etc.).
2. Be a complete game
   * Has a clear goal or win condition (e.g. reach the end, collect X items, beat a timer, escape a room, etc.).
   * Is fully playable from start to finish.
   * Does not rely on obviously placeholder content or broken logic that prevents normal play.
3. Show an honest effort
   * Effort includes (but is not limited to) things like:
     * Multiple designed rooms/areas or levels
     * Thoughtful layout, obstacles, or puzzles
     * Basic polish (e.g. not just an empty scene with a single object)
   * Honest effort is determined solely by Bus.
     * It is at Bus’s sole discretion to disqualify any submission for lack of effort or failure to meet the theme/requirements.

***

### How to Submit

1. Build your game on Portals during the competition window (December 2–23).
2. Publish your game on Portals.
3. Submit your entry using the official submission link: [https://forms.gle/hQtrESG82RDwvvjh7](https://forms.gle/hQtrESG82RDwvvjh7)

Late submissions or edits after the deadline may not be considered.

***

### Judging & Disqualification

* There is no ranked leaderboard for this event; the focus is on participation and creativity.
* Bus will review submissions to confirm:
  * Christmas theme
  * Completeness
  * Honest effort
* Bus may disqualify any submission at their sole discretion, including for:
  * Obvious low-effort or spam entries
  * Games that are incomplete or unplayable
  * Content that is inappropriate or violates Portals/community guidelines

***

### Notes

* The exclusive Christmas wearable is a one-time item for this competition and will be revealed late.
* By entering, you agree that your game and content may be showcased in Portals/community channels.

Get building, have fun, and make something festive.
