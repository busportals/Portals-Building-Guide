### How to setup a Side Scroller Camera

1. Click on the hamburger icon
2. Click Space Options
3. Click Camera
4. Add a new camera state
5. Name your new camera
6. Click on your camera to open the settings
7. _Preferred Settings: Distance -8, Height 2, remaining settings on default_
8. Go back, and change the current camera state to the new one
